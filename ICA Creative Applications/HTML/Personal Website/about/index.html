About Me

Hello, my name is Darren Skidmore! I was born on May 15, 2004 at McKay-Dee Hospital and have lived in Roy my entire life.

I have two parents and no siblings, although I have two dogs that may or may not count. In fact, my whole family is really small; I only have 14 people in my extended family (in the state with the biggest families no less)! I go to WIC full time on A days, and on B days, I split between WIC and Roy High.

I am very logically oriented. Ever since I was a toddler, I have loved numbers and algorithms. Computer programming allows me the best of both worlds. If you would like to know my hobbies, please navigate to this link.

Finally, I am a proud member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It has filled me with great love and joy in my life, family, and community.
